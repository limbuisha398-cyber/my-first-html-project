ABOUT NEPAL

Nepal is one of the most beautiful country of the world which believes unity in diversity.Nepal is honoured by different names like "THE LAND OF HIMALAYA", "THE LAND OF GAUTAM BUDDHA","THE ROOF OF THE WORLD","THE AMAZON OF ASIA" etc. Nepal is the only one country in the world which was never colonized by BRITISH EMPIRE so,Nepal doesn't have its own independence day.

Nepal can offer the breathetaking view of mountains including mount EVEREST. Nepal has 10 of the highest peak of the world out of 14.Nepal is believed to be the most oldest country of south asia having it's own calender which is 35 years faster tha gregorian calender.Nepal has 123 different languages and 125 different ethnic groups.We can imazine that this much of diversity in a country which is 22 times smaller that india and 66 times smaller than china.Many great people all around the world has compared Nepal with heaven in earth."GEOGRAPHY NEWS" has published Nepal as the country with most beautiful landscape.

Inspite of such natural abundance there is a high problem of pollution especially in the city area.Many himalayas were melting day by day,they are losing their beauty. Along with the abundance of natural beauty Nepal has richest history.It is also known as "THE LAND OF GORKHA".Nepali people are famous all around the world because of their kindness,hospitality and simple nature.
